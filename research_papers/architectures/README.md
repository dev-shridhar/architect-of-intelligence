# Architectures Research Papers 🏗️

This folder contains seminal papers focusing on structural advancements in deep learning models and distributed architectures.

## 📄 Papers List

### 1. [Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition (2015)](file:///Users/shridharkulkakrni/.gemini/antigravity-ide/scratch/architect-of-intelligence/research_papers/architectures/resnet_image_recognition.pdf)
*   **Authors**: Kaiming He, Xiangyu Zhang, Shaoqing Ren, Jian Sun.
*   **Impact**: Introduced Residual Networks (ResNet) using skip connections to overcome the vanishing gradient problem, enabling neural networks to scale to hundreds of layers.

### 2. [BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers (2018)](file:///Users/shridharkulkakrni/.gemini/antigravity-ide/scratch/architect-of-intelligence/research_papers/architectures/bert_language_understanding.pdf)
*   **Authors**: Jacob Devlin, Ming-Wei Chang, Kenton Lee, Kristina Toutanova.
*   **Impact**: Introduced bidirectional training for Transformers, demonstrating state-of-the-art results in language understanding and contextual embedding extraction.

### 3. [ImageNet Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks (2012)](file:///Users/shridharkulkakrni/.gemini/antigravity-ide/scratch/architect-of-intelligence/research_papers/architectures/alexnet_image_classification.pdf)
*   **Authors**: Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, Geoffrey E. Hinton.
*   **Impact**: Often called "AlexNet," this paper proved that training deep convolutional networks on GPUs could vastly outperform traditional computer vision methods, starting the modern deep learning revolution.
